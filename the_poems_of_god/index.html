The Poems of God

The Poems of God are just some poems written by some God.

The owner of the website, asablock, found those poems about 1,000 years ago and decided to share them with the world.

There are currently 3 poems of God listed below:

  • Extension Objects (by Tecreaddiat Consin Listemaides)
  • An Used Provide Unique (by Differritatione Naminecent Jacquarticless)
  • Give NeoFor (by Joel Edire Guyan)
